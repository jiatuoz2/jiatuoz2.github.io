I am Jiatuo "Charlie" Zou, a recent master's graduate from Columbia University and currently a visiting research intern at the [SPICE Lab](https://spice-lab.org/) at Northwestern University.

My research lies in **Human-Computer Interaction (HCI)**, at the intersection of extended reality (XR), wearable computing, multimodal AI, and novel sensing. I am interested in building interactions and systems that leverage implicit human sensing and environmental context — driven by AI innovation — to deliver adaptive user experiences.

I am applying to PhD programs for Fall 2026. If our interests overlap, I would love to connect — email is the best way to reach me.
